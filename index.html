What We Do

Customized Order

We make customized orders as per the client's needs. If you give me a design, we can print saree as per your choice. Please tell your friends & family about it..

Bulk Order

We can generate a large number of products in a short duration. Our production usually reaches 10000 Saree per month. We export our product across India to various fashion stores.

Product & Material

We sell different types of Pure Silk, Matka Silk, Vita silk, Silk-Cotton, Bangalore Silk, Tasar Silk Saree. Our production house are enable with both handloom and power loom machine, and our Saree use both natural and adofil color as per the requirement.
